About Me

Who I Am

Hi, I am Tarun, a student eager to learn and explore the world of full-stack development. I enjoy building interactive user interfaces and coding the backend logic that powers them.

My coding journey started out of curiosity for how websites work, which quickly turned into a passion for modern software engineering.

Learning Focus

Currently, I am focused on mastering the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), along with modern styling frameworks, version control, and API development.

I enjoy working on challenges that bridge the gap between design and functional engineering.

Goals & Vision

My goal is to build highly responsive, secure, and user-centric web applications. I strive to write clean, reusable code and follow best development practices.

I hope to collaborate with other developers on open-source projects and contribute to meaningful technological solutions.

Beyond Coding

When I am not in front of my code editor, I spend my time reading about new tech innovations, working on computer hardware, or gaming.

I believe in maintaining a healthy balance between continuous screen-time learning and physical well-being.
